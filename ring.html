Observer Codes

Your ring learns
your code.

Each Observer Code has a distinct physiological signature. The TRACE-R detects which code is dominant — and when it shifts.

NARC

High-output, assertion-dominant. Physiologically characterised by elevated baseline HR and fast HRV recovery under stress.

YARC

Adaptive, socially-patterned. Longer sleep latency, higher temperature variance, strong REM-to-activity correlation.

SIST

Systems-oriented, precision-focused. Lower resting HR, extended deep sleep phases, tight HRV consistency.

Øll

Withdrawal-patterned, interoceptive. High sleep sensitivity, elevated SpO₂ variance, reactive stress response.
