How it works

The loop.
Broken together.

Every turn, the team faces a real conflict scenario. You discuss, you choose a response — and you live with the consequences. NARC and YARC choices drain the clock. Only SIST fills the bank. The loop is a team problem.

⚡

Draw a Trigger

A real conflict scenario — co-parenting, relationships, workplace, friendship. Read it aloud. The table feels it before anyone responds.

🃏

Choose a Response

Every player draws a response card. The team discusses — then one card is played. NARC and YARC move fast. SIST builds toward the win.

◈

Fill the Zero-Point Bank

Only SIST responses add tokens to the bank. Reach 15 tokens before the Loop Deck runs out — play Zero-Point and the team wins.

Ø

Survive ØLL Events

Threshold moments that shake the whole table. Lose Loop Deck cards and bank tokens — unless someone plays the rare ØLL Held card.

👁

Play Observer Mode

Power cards held in hand. Read the room, predict responses, block ØLL events. Rewards SC Framework literacy — the more you understand the codes, the better you play.

📊

Get your Code Readout

At game end — win or lose — every player receives their Observer Code readout from the Team Tracker. The insight is the point, not just the win.
