What it does

Intelligence on your
finger or wrist.

Both devices track the same core health metrics continuously — no tapping, no opening an app, no thinking about it.

♡

Heart Rate Monitoring

Continuous optical heart rate tracking day and night. Resting baseline, active state, and overnight recovery — all logged automatically.

◑

Sleep Tracking

REM cycle detection, sleep staging, and overnight SpO₂. Wake up knowing exactly how well you recovered — not just how long you slept.

◈

Blood Oxygen (SpO₂)

Passive monitoring of blood oxygen saturation levels — a key marker of respiratory health and athletic readiness.

△

Activity Tracking

Step count, calorie burn, and activity recognition built in. Automatically detects workouts and daily movement patterns.

Titanium & Premium Build

Aerospace-grade titanium ring at just 4g. Square-face watch with AMOLED display. Both built to a standard you'd expect from devices this close to your body.

iOS & Android App

Your data, beautifully visualised. Trends, scores, and daily insights — all delivered through the free companion app. No subscription required.
