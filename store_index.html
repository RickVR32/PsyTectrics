What it does

Intelligence on your
finger.

The TRACE-R tracks three core SC Blueprint™ metrics continuously — no tapping, no opening an app, no thinking about it.

◈

SC Blueprint™ ratio

Your NARC, YARC, SIST, and Øll codes tracked in real time. See which code is dominant and how your behavioural pattern shifts throughout the day.

◎

Awareness Index™

Your live position on the −5 to +5 axis. Zero is Zero-Point — the fully balanced state. The ring tells you how close you are and which direction you're moving.

◇

Drift detection

When the gap between your conscious presentation and subconscious pattern becomes significant, the TRACE-R surfaces a quiet alert. A signal before it becomes behaviour.

Heart rate & SpO₂

Continuous optical heart rate and blood oxygen monitoring. Resting baseline, active state, and overnight recovery — fed directly into your awareness profile.

Sleep & recovery

REM cycle detection, sleep staging, and overnight SpO₂. Wake up knowing exactly how your subconscious layer is operating — not just how long you slept.

Aerospace-grade titanium. 4g. Available in Midnight Black, Arctic Silver, and Champagne Gold. The PT chip mark is micro-etched into every ring.
