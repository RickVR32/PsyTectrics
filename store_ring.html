What it tracks

The PsyTec Health Ring monitors your core health metrics continuously — no tapping, no screen to check, no thinking about it. Just data, always running.

Heart Rate Monitoring

Continuous real-time heart rate tracking day and night. Resting baseline, active state, and overnight recovery — all logged automatically to the companion app.

Passive blood oxygen saturation monitoring. A key marker of respiratory health and recovery — measured continuously on your finger without any input from you.
