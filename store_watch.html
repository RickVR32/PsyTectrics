What it does

More data.
On your wrist.

The PsyTec Health Watch goes beyond passive monitoring — it connects, calls, and gives you a full health dashboard at a glance. Everything tracked. Nothing missed.

♡

Heart Rate Monitoring

Continuous 24-hour heart rate tracking. Real-time readouts on the watch face, full history in the Runmefit app. Resting, active, and overnight all logged.

◈

Blood Oxygen (SpO₂)

All-day blood oxygen saturation monitoring. Accuracy comparable to a dedicated oximeter — displayed live on the watch and tracked over time in the app.

◎

Blood Pressure

Wrist-based blood pressure measurement on demand. Track trends over time and understand your cardiovascular baseline — straight from the watch.

◑

Sleep Monitoring

Automatic overnight sleep tracking. Stages, duration, and quality scored every morning. Wake up knowing exactly how well you recovered.

☏

Bluetooth Calling

Make and receive calls directly from your wrist. Built-in mic and speaker — leave your phone in your pocket and stay connected without breaking stride.

△

MAI Health Score

The MAI Health Assessment System converts your biometric data into a single daily score — giving you a clear read on your physical strength and readiness level.
