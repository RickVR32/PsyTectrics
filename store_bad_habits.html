How it works

Together you
break it.

Bad Habits is fully collaborative — the whole team wins together or loses together. No one gets left out. No one wins at someone else's expense. That's the point.

🃏

Show your hand

On your turn, show the table your cards. Talk about them together. Which one should you play? The best decision is a team decision.

✨

Play Good Habits

Good Habit cards add tokens to the Good Habits Jar. These are your best friends. Play them whenever you can — they build toward the win.

⏱

Watch the Habit Deck

Every Bad Habit or Habit Loop card played removes tokens from the countdown. When they're gone — everyone loses. Play smart.

💡

Ask The Question

Ask! cards must have their question read aloud before playing. Curiosity is always a positive move. The whole team can answer — and earn bonus tokens.

🎉

Fill the jar,
Break it!

Reach 15 Good Habit tokens in the jar and play the Break The Habit! card. Game over — the whole team wins and celebrates together.

🌟

Watch for
Habit Loop Held

The rare green Habit Loop Held card — someone spotted a bad habit coming and stopped it. Big cheer for that player. Plus 2 bonus tokens!
