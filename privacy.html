Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · Version 1.0

1. Who we are

PsyTectrics Technologies Ltd is a company registered in England and Wales. We operate the website psytectrics.com and provide psychometric assessment and behavioural analysis services. For data protection purposes, we are the data controller.

Contact: psytectrics.com/contact

2. What data we collect

Depending on how you use our services, we may collect:

  • Assessment data — your responses to the 56-question SC Blueprint™ assessment
  • Report request data — name, contact details, and any content you submit when commissioning a report
  • Observer Report submissions — third-party behavioural descriptions or message data you submit for analysis
  • Payment data — handled entirely by Stripe; we do not store card details
  • Contact form submissions — name, email, and message content
  • Technical data — IP address, browser type, and usage data collected via standard server logs

3. How we use your data

  • To generate and deliver your assessment profile or report
  • To respond to enquiries and support requests
  • To process payments (via Stripe)
  • To improve our services and assessment methodology
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising.

4. Legal basis for processing (UK GDPR)

  • Contract — processing necessary to deliver a service you have purchased or requested
  • Legitimate interests — improving our products and communicating with customers
  • Legal obligation — where required by law

5. Observer Report data

When you submit an Observer Report request, you are providing data about a third party. By submitting, you confirm that you have a lawful basis to share this data and that doing so does not violate any applicable law or court order. Submitted third-party data is used solely to generate your requested report and is not retained beyond 90 days from delivery.

6. Data retention

  • Assessment responses: retained for up to 12 months to support profile retrieval and service continuity
  • Observer Report submissions: deleted within 90 days of report delivery
  • Contact and enquiry data: retained for up to 24 months
  • Payment records: retained as required by HMRC (typically 6 years)

7. Third parties

We use the following third-party processors:

  • Stripe — payment processing (stripe.com/gb/privacy)
  • Formspree — form submission handling
  • Anthropic — AI-powered report generation (reports are generated via API; no data is used to train models under our agreement)

8. Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Data portability
  • Lodge a complaint with the ICO (ico.org.uk)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via our contact page.

9. Cookies

This site uses only essential session-level cookies required for the assessment flow to function. No tracking, advertising, or analytics cookies are set.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The version date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of our services after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
